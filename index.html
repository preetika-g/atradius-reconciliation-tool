User Guide
Monthly reconciliation workflow — step by step.
Monthly workflow
1

Upload collections

Go to Collections. Select the Atradius invoices CSV and click Upload collections. Rows are appended — safe to upload multiple months.

2

Upload checkouts

Go to Checkouts. Select the Checkouts to Atradius CSV and click Upload checkouts. Duplicate checkout IDs are automatically skipped.

3

Run reconciliation

Go to Run & Results. Select a month or All periods. Keep threshold at 80%. Click Run reconciliation.

4

Copy IDs & update tracker

Click Copy reconciled checkout IDs. Then go to Update Tracker, open the Google Sheet, and paste (Ctrl+V) into the Checkout ID column.

5

Review Gaps & Flags

Check the orange panel — contact Atradius for no-reference collections. Review unmatched collections and open checkouts.

6

Save your data

Click Save to browser in the header. Use Restore saved next session.
